## What do we do?
The Victoria Gallery has multiple events around the year, where we do not fail to setup a stall eagerly waiting to introduce the world to our world of microbes. 

We introduced excited young children and keen parents to the astonishing diversity of the microbial world, right from the bacteria on their hands to those thriving in their stomachs and across the environment. We explored the importance of the ‘good’ microbes and the risks of the ‘bad’ ones. We introduced a pipetting challenge for kids, showing them how to use pipettes and multi-chanel pipettes using blue-dye, and saw live DNA sequencing in action with the Oxford Nanopore MinION. A “design your own microbe” station rounded out the session, blending creativity with microbiology and offering a glimpse into how science is both discovered and imagined.
